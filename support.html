FAQ

Does JFlutter work offline?

Yes. JFlutter includes bundled examples and core educational workflows that run locally without an account or developer-operated network service.

Where are my files stored?

Imported and exported files are handled through user-initiated file picker and export flows. Files are opened from or saved to locations you choose.

Does JFlutter collect analytics or tracking data?

No. JFlutter does not collect analytics, use tracking SDKs, show ads, or send your app content to developer-operated servers. See the Privacy Policy.
